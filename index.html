Everything we offer

Two licensed streams — plumbing and HVAC — coordinated so you are not bounced between unrelated contractors.

Plumbing Residential & light commercial

Emergency & repairs

Burst pipes, active leaks, failed PRVs, running toilets, backed-up fixtures, sump failures, hose bib freeze breaks, and slab-area moisture investigation with isolation testing.

Drain & sewer

Snake and auger clearing, hydro-jetting where appropriate, mainline camera with locator, spot repair vs. line replacement guidance, backwater valve assessment, and cleanout installation planning.

Water heaters

Tank and tankless sizing, gas and electric connections, recirculation pumps, mixing valves, expansion tanks, anode service, flush & burn maintenance, and warranty-preserving installs.

Fixtures & finishes

Faucets, showers, tubs, garburators, dishwashers, ice lines, laundry boxes, outdoor silcocks, and accessibility-focused lever and thermostatic upgrades.

Water quality & treatment

Softeners, iron filters, UV, RO drinking systems, pressure-reducing valves, whole-home filtration, and hardness testing for Edmonton region water chemistry.

Renovations & rough-in

Basement bars, secondary suites (where permitted), kitchen and bath relocations, hydronic tie-ins with mechanical partners, gas line to fireplace or range (coordinated with HVAC/gas fitters as required).

HVAC Heating, cooling & air quality

Furnaces

High-efficiency replacement, heat exchanger inspection, ignition and flame-sensor service, blower cleaning, duct static pressure checks, and carbon monoxide safety verification.

Air conditioning

Central AC install and replacement, refrigerant leak search, coil cleaning, TXV and contactor service, seasonal start-up and winterization.

Heat pumps

Cold-climate heat pump sizing, dual-fuel with furnace backup, defrost diagnostics, line set routing for aesthetic and efficiency, and rebate documentation assistance.

IAQ & controls

Humidifiers, HRV/ERV, media filters, UV treatment, smart thermostats, zoning discussions, and airflow balancing for hot upper floors common in newer Southside builds.

Garage & specialty

Unit heaters, radiant coordination, make-up air for tight envelopes, and ventilation for workshops and detached garages.

Maintenance plans

Seasonal furnace and AC tune-ups, priority booking, discounted diagnostics, and documented service history for home resale.
