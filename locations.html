Capital Region coverage

Locations we serve

Edmonton is our hub. We dispatch certified plumbing and HVAC technicians across the extended metro and surrounding towns. Target 1-hour ETA applies after dispatch confirmation, subject to conditions on the ground.

Call-out rate outside Edmonton proximity

For properties located more than approximately 15 minutes’ drive from Edmonton (as determined by dispatch routing at time of booking), a $150.00 call-out fee applies in addition to quoted work. This fee covers mobilization and initial on-site assessment; we disclose it before we roll.
